About Moodmate

Our mission and the team behind the app

Our Mission

Moodmate was created by a team of mental health professionals, AI specialists, and college students who recognized the need for accessible mental health support on campus. Our mission is to empower students to prioritize their mental well-being through technology that understands and adapts to their unique needs.

We believe that by combining artificial intelligence with evidence-based mental health practices, we can provide personalized support that helps students build resilience, develop healthy coping strategies, and thrive during their college years.
